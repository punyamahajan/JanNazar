## 💡 The Problem

Cities still rely on slow, manual complaint systems where citizens fill lengthy forms, duplicate reports flood departments, and complaints often disappear into a black box with little visibility.

Citizens don't know:

* Has my complaint reached the right department?
* Is anyone working on it?
* Why was it prioritised?
* When will it be resolved?

---

# ✨ Our Solution

**JanNazar** is an AI-powered civic intelligence platform that allows citizens to report civic issues using **voice, images, or text in any language**.

Every report is processed through a transparent **Agentic AI pipeline** that automatically:

* 🎤 Understands multilingual speech
* 📷 Analyses uploaded images
* 🌍 Translates local languages
* 🧠 Classifies civic issues
* 🔍 Detects duplicate complaints
* 🏛 Routes reports to the correct department
* 🚨 Calculates issue priority

Unlike conventional systems, JanNazar lets citizens **watch the AI reason in real time**, building trust through transparency instead of hiding decisions behind a loading spinner.
